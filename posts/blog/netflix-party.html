What is and How to Use Netflix Party / Teleparty

06/08/2022

Description

Netflix Party (or Teleparty) is a simple web extension for Chrome, Edge, and Firefox that became popular during the 2020 coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic. The extension allows you to watch Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, and much more while you are not physically with them. The extension syncs your video with other people in the "party" and allows for a live chat and emoji reactions feature so your friends can comment on the video while it is playing. It also boasts features where if a person pauses or rewinds the video, the video on all other peoples videos will rewind and play at the same time. This is a great extension if you are looking to watch an online video with a friend who is not physically with you.


Netflix Party was recently renamed to Teleparty, all online articles referring to Netflix party carry along to Teleparty. However teleparty allows you to also watch videos with your friends on sites such as Hulu, Amazon Prime, Disney+, and much more.
